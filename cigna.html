overview

Cigna Health realized that there customers had a difficult time understanding and navigating their provider benefits during their healthcare journey. They needed to provide their customers with answers, before they had questions, with solutions before they realized there was a problem.

As the Senior UX Designer, my task was to craft a “sensing” experience around integrated messaging and virtual assistance, an extension of their OneGuide platform. Working with Citizen Plus and Cigna Health, we provided Cigna customers with contextual guidance anticipated their needs, and relevant resources, during their healthcare journey.

Interacting and interviewing over 40 patients suffering from musculoskeletal back-pain with various health care providers, led us to understand their situations and possible touch-points based on available provider data. With the improved OneGuide experience, participants saw their needs being met. They were offered less-expensive alternate medications, reminders about their physical, mental, and massage therapy beneifts, and resources to help them prep for surgery.
