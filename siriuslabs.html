Nigeria has a lot of problems with technology development: two of them being linked to poor quality and scam-artists. People here will say that they can develop android apps, websites, iOS apps, AI tools, and other kinds of basic to advanced software: when in reality, they have no idea what they're doing. They'll properly sell to clients, and end up running away with someone else's hard earned money. This is creating a poor reputation for Nigerian agencies and development shops worldwide.
