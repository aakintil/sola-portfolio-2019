overview

Lead the design of the interactive prototype that Fedex and Ziba Design used to validate the newer version of the PowerPad. Assited in research and gathering feedback about couriers' negative experiences with the existing Powerpad, and then testing proposed workflows. Ensuring that our ideas were validated before rolling out the entire platfom, we focused on low-volume, delivery-only couriers, the first-overnight couriers.

The original Powerpad was developed and designed around constraints and biases that are no longer valid. Built in a monolithic fashion using Windows Forms, the system followed rigid linear paths, constricting employees and forcing them to follow workflows that blocked them from performing specific tasks. With Fedex, we provided alternative and modern solution to the existing workflows, software, and hardware.
